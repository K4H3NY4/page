The COVID 19 pandemic came with changes that none had anticipated as we got into the year 2020. Some of the people affected most by these changes are the small business owners. Being your local merchants, the new directives imposed by goverments meant that their revenue streams were going to reduce, and reduce they did.

We thus saws the need for a brand like VISA to remind people of the important role these small businesses play in our lives and why it was vital for all of us to keep supporting them.
